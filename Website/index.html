About
Shamsuddin Refaei

CONTACT - SEND ME AN EMAIL

I'm a 20 year old photographer living in Ireland with a passion for cars and cameras. I bought my first camera at 16 and since then have been slowly developing my skills and techniques.

My interest in photography started when I was 16 years old, seeing photos online taken by photographers of different places across the world. Since then, I've been amazed by the idea of capturing something you see and sharing it with the world.

My passion for cars also started when I was young. I've always loved the idea of modifying a car to make it your own and taking pride in your build. I combined my passion for cars and photography to produce works of art to share with people across the world.
